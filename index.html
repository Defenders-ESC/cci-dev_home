About Us

The goal of the Center for Conservation Innovation is to find innovative ways to improve biological conservation. Finding, analyzing, and making available data on ESA-listed species - and species not-yet listed - is one way we are working towards that goal.

This site is our web application development testbed. Feel free to explore the content in this site and give us feedback.

Nothing on this site should be construed as representing the positions of Defenders of Wildlife; content here is a work in progress that may be subject to change.

Web apps, analyses, working papers, and related tools

Web Apps

A selection of our web apps. Those labeled Restricted are under active research and development; see → Contact Us to inquire about access.

Section 7 Explorer

FWS consultation data

Section 7 of the U.S. Endangered Species Act is often considered the strongest section of the Act. Explore data on all section 7 consultations performed by the U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service since 2008.

Dynamic Recovery

Web-based recovery plans

Recovery plans set the criteria for ESA-listed species to be considered recovered and detail the actions needed to achieve those criteria. Traditional recovery plans are written on paper, and most are out-of-date. These are models of how recovery plans could be modernized.

ESAdocs Search

Full-text search engine

Full-text search engine for ESA documents. Includes all Federal Register notices, recovery plans, and 5-year reviews, and a variety of section 7 consultations, HCPs, and other ESA-related documents.

ESA Listings Summary

Overview of ESA listings

The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service provides a table of Endangered Species Act-listed species from their ECOS portal. Explore the same data using graphs and maps to learn more about ESA-listed species.

ESA Expenditures

How much money, where?

Conservation takes resources. The U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service annually reports expenditures on ESA-listed species. Explore reported expenditure data from 2008-2013 in maps, graphs, and tables.

Working Lands for Wildlife

NRCS wildlife program

The Natural Resources Conservation Service administers the Working Lands for Wildlife program to provide funding and assistance to private landowners whose land management can help species that are ESA-listed or candidates for listing. Explore WLFW data here!

At-risk Species Explorer

Petitioned/candidate species

A significant number of species are proposed for ESA listing or are candidates. Explore basic information about these imperiled species. This is an early prototype; please contact us if you would like to help develop.

Endangered Species Images

Image collection tool

Having pictures of ESA-listed species should help improve our ability communicate about their conservation. This will be an app to crowd-source image curation.

Recovery Actions

Actions to recover species

Recovery plans detail the actions needed to improve a species' status to the point that the protections of the Endangered Species Act are no longer needed. Graphically explore >30,000 actions for 1,178 species from the ROAR database.

FWS Listing Workplan

FWS's 7-year workplan

FWS released their seven-year workplan for evaluating >350 species for listing under the ESA. We extracted the data from their PDF to make it more accessible and useable.

ESA 5-year Reviews

Timeliness of 5-yr reviews

The US Endangered Species Act requires a review of the status of listed species once every five years. This is a simple tool to view the timeliness of five-year reviews as of early 2017.

Analyses

An occasional series of short analyses that inform conservation efforts on a wide array of topics.

ESA Funding

Needs and appropriations

An analysis of the funding needs for threatened and endangered species versus appropriations and spending.

Section 7 Take

ESA-authorized harm

An analysis of data on "take" (i.e., harm) authorized during 7 consultation by . The data were collected by as part of their consultation program.

Working Papers

An occasional series of short "papers" discussing our ongong work (and hence differentiated from Analyses).

Section 7 Coordinates

Where coordinates are recorded

The US Fish and Wildlife Service has only recorded geographic coordinates for ~1/2 of section 7 consultations since 2008. Here we examine the factors for predicting which consultations have coordinates recorded.

Recovery Actions (v1)

Recovery actions analysis

A brief overview of the FWS and NMFS recovery action database, ROAR. Presented in a flex dashboard to test the format.

Recovery Actions (v2)

Recovery actions analysis

A brief overview of the FWS and NMFS recovery action database, roar. Presented with Tufte theme to test the format.

Section 7 Take

ESA-authorized harm

A brief overview of authorized 'take' (i.e., harm) statements recorded in FWS's section 7 database; uses a flexdashboard format.

Section 10 Agreements (v1)

Non-federal conservation agmts

A brief overview of agreements made under section 10(a)(1)(B) of the ESA.

Section 10 Agreements (v2)

Non-federal conservation agmts

An alternative analysis of agreements made under section 10(a)(1)(B) of the ESA.

Critical Habitat Changes

Critical habitat 2001-2011

An initial examination of how habitat types changed in designated critical habitat for 42 species, 2001-2011.

Recovery Plan Times

Recovery plan timeliness

An overview of how many species have recovery plans, how long planning took, and the age of current plans.

Shiny Microapps

A set of small Shiny apps that provide a single, focused service, to be used as embeds (iframes) in non-Shiny webpages/sites.

Section 7 Microapp

Section 7 summary figures

Three basic bargraphs of section 7 data, with a link to the Section 7 Explorer app. Species selection is specified as a query string in the URL.

GBIF Map Microapp

Global Biodiv. Info. Facility

A very basic Leaflet map with occurrence records from GBIF. Species selection is specified as a query string in the URL.

Threats & Demography

Status score display

A microapp illustrating how threat status and demographic status of imperiled species could be displayed.

Threats Relative Importance

Record and display threats

A microapp illustrating how the relative importance of threats could be gathered (e.g., through expert elicitation) and displayed.

Recovery Action Table

ROAR recovery action table

A microapp displaying a table of recover actions from FWS's ROAR. Species selection is specified as a query string in the URL.

ESA Policy Annotations

A section for group annotations of proposed ESA policies using Hypothes.is. At the moment, annotations require using the Chrome plugin for Hypothes.is.

HCP Handbook Revisions

Proposed HCP handbook

FWS and NMFS have drafted an update to their HCP handbook. This copy of the draft revised handbook is set up for annotation.
